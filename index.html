01 The Soul of Remezzo

Our Story

Mornings begin barefoot — Greek coffee under the vines, fishing boats at the quay, warm sand a few steps from your table.

By afternoon, meze and a chilled tsipouro. By night, lantern light and the glow of the old port. No rush, no noise — just the island, making you feel at home.

09—01Dawn to Midnight
09:00 — Morning

The Beach Kafeneío

Sun-warmed sand, sea-blue chairs and frappé over ice. Slow breakfasts and salt air, the way the island has always taken its mornings.

18:00 — Golden Hour

Sun on the Water

The light turns to amber, an Aperol catches the glow, and meze plates appear as the whole promenade exhales into evening.

21:00 — Night

The Harbour Glenti

Lanterns flicker over candlelit tables, the boats shimmer on the water, and music drifts across the old port long after the stars appear.
